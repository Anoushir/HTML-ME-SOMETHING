"Below is a list of vehicles I've owned since my first purchase till 2017."

Vehicles:

  • 1992 Plymouth Laser RS
  • 1997 Lincoln Mark VIII
  • 2002 Chevy Camaro Z/28
  • 1997 Mitsubishi Eclipse GSX

Motorcycles:

  • 1982 Honda CX500
  • 1976 Honda CB550
  • 2008 Suzuki GSX-R 600
  • 2017 Yamaha FZ-09

1992 Plymouth Laser

"The 1992 Plymouth Laser was technically both my third and sixth car. The first being bought aroung age 19. Lacking automotive knowledge I learned to work specifcally on DSM's (Diamond Star Motors). The second Laser I purchased at age 25. Now equipped with knowledge and a little more money, I built the car I still own today, Falecia. Pictured below went 11.63 @123mph. Since has gained a larger HX40 turbo and has been swapped to automatic transmission.

1997 Lincoln Mark VIII

"In September of 2005 I finally had turned 16 and was on the search for my first car. Though the Lincoln held a 4.6 liter V8 engine, it was not the ideal car for a race spirited 16 year old. However I learned a lot about this platform. Ultimately this car introduced me to the realm of the internet. I accessed forums to help diagnose issues I was having. In the end with very basic bolt ons the car went 14.8 @97mph. I was ecstatic at the time."

2002 Chevy Camaro Z/28

"I had sold all of my 'race cars' and just graduating from automotive trade school in Chicago. I was on the lookout for a new car, I wanted something new entirely. I found a fourth generation LS1 Camaro. It had 68k miles and was as clean as they come. The car was mostly built by the previous owner. Consisted of a fairly large head/cam combination. Eventually this car went 11.64 @ 122mph. Which at the time was the fastest car I'd ever owned or driven."

1997 Mitsubishi Eclipse GSX

"My second car was a 1997 Mitsubishi Eclipse. With this car I learned a lot about forced induction, maintenance, and traction. This car was not great to me however many times the issues were operator error. This was my first Turbo Charged vehicle also my first of many 4g63 engine. The engine I would base my automotive racing path apon."

2007 Suzuki GSX-R 600

The 2007 Suzuki GSX-R was an incredible machine. It was stunning, a perfect white and blue combination , graphics removed. Great sound and even better performance. The Suzuki opened my eyes to what would eventually be another passion of mine. Twisty roads and perfect apex's

1982 Honda CX-500

An all around awesome machine. The CX500 was the first V twin motor in a Honda motorcycle. My wife Katie purchased this Honda for me on my 25th birthday. At the time I liked the style of cafe racers and had plans to mold the bike into something simlar. Plans changed drastically over the years. I ended up with a mix of all sorts. But an absolute blast to ride and gets attention everywhere I take it.

1976 Honda CB550

The most recent build is my 1976 Honda CB550. Purchased recently as a project for my wife Katie. The hope is to restore the bike to a reliable good looking cruiser. One she can learn to ride on and still be enough to not grow out of immediately. Still work to be done on this one.

2017 Yamaha FZ-09

My most recent purchase is this 2017 Yamaha FZ-09. Best performing bike I've ever had the priveladge to ride. Triple cylinder 845cc engine delivers over 120hp and 76ftlb. of torque. I've done minor modifications but have only owned the bike a short time. More to come.
